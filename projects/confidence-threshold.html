Confidence Threshold

2023

Photography

Confidence Threshold interrogates the point at which accumulated visual evidence becomes sufficient for belief. The work examines the mechanisms — statistical, perceptual, institutional — by which photographic images cross from uncertainty into accepted truth. Each piece in the series occupies the margin where data has not yet become knowledge, where the image is still negotiating its own credibility.
