Confidence Threshold

2023

Photography

Confidence Threshold interrogates the point at which accumulated visual evidence becomes sufficient for belief. The work examines the mechanisms — statistical, perceptual, institutional — by which photographic images cross from uncertainty into accepted truth. Each piece in the series occupies the margin where data has not yet become knowledge, where the image is still negotiating its own credibility.

Perceptual Boundaries

threshold studies

Studies that isolate the moment where accumulated visual information begins to coalesce into belief — graduated tones and subtle shifts that mark the boundary between uncertainty and conviction.

Institutional Markers

verification + credibility

Images examining the institutional mechanisms that confer credibility — the marks, stamps, and visual codes by which photographic evidence crosses from ambiguity into accepted fact.
